Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-06 · App: PitPace (Android) · Publisher: Acme Speedworks Srl · Contact: am@andreamaselli.com

Summary

PitPace runs entirely on your phone. It has no servers, no account, and no cloud. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data. Your settings, track profiles, pit-stop history and error log stay on your device and are deleted when you clear the app data or uninstall it. PitPace contains no ads and no third-party analytics or tracking.

1. Information we process (on your device only)

  • Location (GPS): used in real time to detect crossing the pit-entry / pit-exit lines, to measure speed, and to time the pit stop. With your permission it also runs in the background (with an ongoing notification) to detect pit entry when the screen is off. Your location is never sent off the device and is not stored as a history of where you have been.
  • App settings & track profiles, pit-stop history, error log: saved in local storage on the device.

We do not collect names, emails, contacts, identifiers, advertising IDs, or analytics.

2. How we use it

Solely to provide the app's functionality (pit-lane timing, gate detection, speed alarm, guidance, diagnostics) on your device. There is no other use.

3. Sharing

We do not share your information with anyone. There are no third-party services, ad networks or analytics providers in the app. If you manually export your tracks, history or error log and send the file to someone, that transfer is initiated and controlled by you.

4. Storage & retention

All data is stored locally and persists until you clear the app's data/storage or uninstall the app, which deletes it. We hold no copy.

5. Permissions

  • Location (precise/approximate, incl. background): to detect the pit-lane gates and time the stop, including with the screen off. Required for the core function.
  • Foreground service & notifications: to keep GPS active and show the ongoing notification while timing in the background (Android requirement).

You can revoke location permission anytime in Android Settings; the GPS features will then stop working.

6. Children

PitPace is a motorsport tool not directed at children and does not knowingly process children's data.

7. Your rights

Because all data stays on your device and we hold none, you are in full control: view it in the app, export it, or delete it by clearing app data / uninstalling. Questions: am@andreamaselli.com.

8. Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will change. Material changes are noted in the app's release notes.

9. Safety notice

PitPace is a driver/engineer aid, not a certified timing device. Do not operate the screen while driving; use the volume-button markers or have a co-driver / pit-wall engineer operate it.
